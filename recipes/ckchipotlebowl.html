Chicken Chipotle Bowl

Description

The chicken chipotle bowl, made popular by the rapidly expanding Chipotle restaurant, is the perfect meal that blends flavor, health, and accessibility of ingredients. The combination of protein from the chicken, complex carbohydrates from the rice and beans, and assortment of vitamins from the various vegetables allows for not only an flavor-filled experience, but a super healthy and convenient meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 bundle of cilantro
  • 2 limes
  • 8 ounces of chicken breast
  • 3 ounces of black or pinto beans
  • 1 whole tomato
  • Half a red onion
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • Adobada marinade
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut up chicken breast into bite-sized cubes. Place into a mixing bowl and mix in adobada marinade and juice of 1 lime.
  2. Let chicken rest in fridge for 10 minutes.
  3. While chicken rests, begin to roughly chop up cilantro. Dice up tomato and red onion into small pieces.
  4. Mix half of the chopped cilantro with the entirety of the tomatoes and red onion. Squeeze half a lime and mix again. After mixing, set aside in fridge.
  5. Remove marinated chicken from fridge. Lightly coat a pan to medium-high heat. Place chicken onto pan and let cook for 4 minutes per side. After each flip, lightly glaze pieces of chicken with leftover marinade from bowl.
  6. While chicken is cooking, place 1 cup of cooked rice into a mixing bowl. Dump the other half of cilantro into bowl and squeeze the last half of lime over the rice. Add salt to taste, then fully incorporate the rice together.
  7. When chicken is finished cooking, take pan off heat and let rest for 4 minutes.
  8. Rinse and drain cooked beans of choice.
  9. Plate rice and beans into a bowl, adding the rested chicken atop. Place the pico de gallo on top of the chicken.
  10. Take a photo for the Gram and enjoy!
